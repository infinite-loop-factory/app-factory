## Full Description
Ever wondered how many countries you've visited? Country Tracker automatically records your travels and shows them on a beautiful interactive globe.

**Automatic Tracking**
No manual logging needed. Country Tracker detects when you cross borders and records your visits automatically in the background.

**Interactive Map**
View all your visited countries on a 3D globe. Filter by year or date range to see your travel history at a glance.

**Travel Statistics**
See how many countries you've visited, total days abroad, and your most frequent destinations.

**Smart Visit Entry**
Add past visits manually by selecting a photo — we'll extract the location and date from the image metadata.

**Privacy First**
Your data is encrypted and only accessible to you. No third-party sharing, no ads.

**Features:**
- Automatic background country detection
- Interactive 3D globe (iOS) and 2D map (web)
- Filter travels by year or custom date range
- Add visits from photos (EXIF data)
- Travel statistics and insights
- Multi-language support (English, Korean)
- Light and dark themes
- Export and delete your data anytime
